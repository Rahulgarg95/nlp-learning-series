# Real-World NLP Text Difficulties – Examples from Various Domains

1. Unicode and Encoding Noise
----------------------------
Café, naïve, résumé, coöperate, jalapeño, São Paulo
Invisible chars: zero-width space, bidirectional control \u200e\u202e
Multilingual chars mixing Latin+Arabic+Cyrillic: مرحبا, привет!

2. Web and Social Media Noise
-----------------------------
URLs: Check out https://example.com now! Or ftp://fileserver.local
Emails: Contact me at john.doe@example.org :-)
Hashtags & Mentions: #NLProcks @openai
Repeated characters & emotive punctuation: sooooo excited!!! 😂😂😂
Slang, abbreviations: LMAO, idk, brb, gonna, wanna, gotta

3. Emojis and Symbols
---------------------
Smileys 😄, animals 🐱, flags 🇺🇸🇧🇷, math symbols ∑∫≈∞
Emoji sequences combining skin tones and modifiers 👩🏽‍💻👨🏻‍🚀

4. Code, Logs, and Technical Text
---------------------------------
Code snippets: print("Hello, world!")
Logs: ERROR [2023-08-15 15:32:10] NullPointerException at line 42
Commands: $ pip install -r requirements.txt

5. Abbreviations and Domain Slang
---------------------------------
Medical: Pt (patient), Rx (prescription), BP (blood pressure), QID (4 times daily)
Legal: Affidavit, subpoena, prima facie, certiorari
Finance: ROI, EBITDA, CAGR

6. Dates, Times, and Numerics
-----------------------------
Time formats: 14:30, 2:30pm, midnight
Dates: 2023-07-21, 21/07/2023, July 21st, '21
Numbers: 3.14, -27, 1,000 or 1 000

7. Mixed Languages and Code-Switching
-------------------------------------
"Hola amigos, I will meet you mañana."
"Vamos to the park after work."
Handling scripts: English (Latin), Devanagari, Arabic, Chinese

8. Special Formatting and Markup
--------------------------------
HTML tags: <div>Hello</div>, <a href="link">
Markdown: **bold**, *italic*, `code`
LaTeX math: $E=mc^2$, \int_a^b f(x) dx

9. Noise from OCR and Scanned Text
----------------------------------
Misrecognized chars: "l" vs "1", "rn" vs "m"
Smudges leading to missing letters

10. Privacy and Sensitive Data
------------------------------
Personal names, emails masked: <PERSON>, <EMAIL>
Locations, dates replaced: <LOCATION>, <DATE>

11. Contracted Words and Fixing
-------------------------------
I'm -> I am
don't -> do not
can't -> cannot
gonna -> going to

12. Whitespace and Punctuation Challenges
-----------------------------------------
Multiple spaces/tabs, line breaks in middle of sentences
Punctuations as sentiment indicators e.g., "?!", "...", "!!!"
